# Barbarian

The Barbarian is a Legendary level tank hero in the Horde faction, specializing in tanking, controlling enemies, and debuffs.
Hero Shards unlock in the Tavern at recruitment Lv. 18
[[File:|style="border-radius: 30px;"]]BarbarianRacer Class:Horde

## Skills


### Fortified Defense

Description:Grant the hero a shield that can absorb damage up to [x]% of its current HP and reduce the Attack Speed of nearby enemies by 20% for 6 seconds. Each time damage is taken, 40% of it can be absorbed by the shield. Cooldown: 10 seconds.
- ★ Shield absorption is increased by 5%. While the shield is active, gain an extra 25% Damage Reduction.
- ★★ Shield absorption increases by 5%.
- ★★★ Shield absorption is additionally increased by 5%. While the shield is active, gain an extra 25% Damage Reduction on Normal Attacks.

### Taunt

Description:Ultimate Skill: Taunt 3 enemies, forcing them to attack this hero for 4 seconds and lowering their Attack by [x]%. Cooldown: 14 seconds.
- ★ Attack reduction effect is increased by 5%, duration is extended by 1 second, affects 1 more target.
- ★★ Attack reduction effect is increased by 5%.
- ★★★ Attack reduction effect is increased by 5%, duration is extended by 1 second, affects 1 more target.

### Insatiable Hunger

Description:Increase the HP by [x]% and Attack by [x]%.
- ★ Boosts HP bonus by an additional 5%.
- ★★ Attack bonus is increased by 5%.
- ★★★ Gain an additional 5% damage reduction.

### Savage Strike

Description:Smash the front, inflicting [x]% damage and stunning up to 3 enemies for 1.5 seconds. Cooldown: 6 seconds.
- ★ Skill damage is additionally increased by 15%. After a successful hit, gain 15% Critical Resistance for 5 seconds.
- ★★ Skill damage increases by 15%.
- ★★★ Skill damage is additionally increased by 15%. Deals true damage equal to 4% of the max HP, up to 400% of the Attack.

### Horde Defender

Description:When deployed, the Damage Reduction of all Horde Heroes in the Queue increases by [x]%.
- ★ Damage Reduction bonus increases by an additional 2.5%.
- ★★ Damage Reduction bonus increases by an additional 2.5%.
- ★★★ Barbarian gains stun immunity.

## Gear Skills

Exclusive Skill: In battle, marks an enemy hero, increasing damage dealt by your Horde heroes to the target by 15%. When the marked target falls, a new target is marked immediately.
- Lv. 5: At the start of battle, boost this hero's HP by 20%
- Lv. 10: Taunt and Intimidation from Barbarian stuns marked heroes for 2.5 seconds when they land.
- Lv. 15: At the beginning of the battle, boost all your Horde heroes' Damage Reduction by 10%.
- Lv. 20: Marked heroes gain 1 Shatter stacks from your Horde heroes' attacks. At 40 stacks, the target suffers damage equal to 20% of the max HP (capped at 800% of the Barbarian's Attack), resetting the stack count.

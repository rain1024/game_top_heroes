# Warlock

Warlockis a powerful backline DPS (Damage Per Second) hero inTop Heroes, known for his devastating area-of-effect spells and curse-based magic. He thrives in long battles where his DoT (Damage over Time) abilities can wear enemies down while amplifying damage taken by foes.

## Lore

A former scholar of forbidden arts, Warlock sacrificed his soul in exchange for limitless magical power. His twisted chants and dark incantations channel shadowflame and chaos, tearing through enemy ranks with merciless precision. Few dare face him without warding spells or protection from the arcane.

## Skills and Abilities

1. Hellfire Circle (Ultimate) 🔥
Warlock summons a circle of flame that deals massive AoE damage and applies a burn effect for 3 turns.
→ Damage scales with Warlock's INT. Burn stacks with other fire heroes.
2. Curse of Weakness 🧿
Curses all enemies, reducing their DEF and RES for 2 turns.
→ Pairs well with high-burst heroes who follow up immediately.
3. Soul Leech 🌑
Warlock steals HP from the enemy with the lowest health.
→ Good sustain skill in PvE dungeon grinds.
4. Dark Offering (Passive) ☠️
Each enemy death boosts Warlock’s INT by 5% (stacks up to 5 times per battle).
→ Synergizes well in minion-heavy waves or with cleave-based teams.

## Formation & Strategy

- Best Position: Backline (Slot 5 or 6)
- Synergies:🔥Pyromancer– Amplifies fire synergy⚡Stormcaller– Takes advantage of lowered RES from Curse💀Necromancer– Combos well in undead or DOT-themed teams
- Counters: Warlock is vulnerable to silence or energy drain (e.g. fromClericorSpellbreakerenemies).

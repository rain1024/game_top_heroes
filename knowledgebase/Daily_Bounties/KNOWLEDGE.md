# Daily Bounties


## Bounty Quests

Bounty quests reset every8 hoursand provide excellent rewards. The higher your bounty level, the better the rewards. These can be found in theBounty Hall.

## Bounty Bosses

- Faction: League
- Soldier Faction: Horde

### Nadine

- Faction: League
- Soldier Faction: Horde

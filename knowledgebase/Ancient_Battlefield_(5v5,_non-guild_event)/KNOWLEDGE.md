# Ancient Battlefield (5v5, non-guild event)

Also called the Ancient Battlefield, this is the version of the Ancient Battlefield event that does not include the entire guild.The Glory Battlefield or Ancient Battlefield (non-guild) is an event in which two teams of five from all different servers are paired together to claim facilities and gather resources to gain the most points over the opposing team.Points gained in this event are Combat Valor, which are redeemed in the points store.StoryThis event is both a test of power and a challenge of strategy. Lords must skillfully pick and match their heroes to overcome trials, defeat mighty enemies, and claim the ancient battlefield's rewards. This will all become part of their legend on this enchanted continent.How to PlayLords with castles level 20 and above can participate in this event. Once the battle starts, multiple time slots will be available for matchmaking.Two teams are matched into the same battlefield. Capture as many buildings as possible to earn points. Each time consists of 5 lords.The team that first captures or continuously holds facilities will earn a large number of Battle Points, and the team with the most points at the end wins.Upon entering the battlefield, soldiers will be assigned based on the lords' barracks and training ground levels, and soldiers will not die.During the battle, lords will receive a free relocation opportunity at regular intervals.Killing soldiers in the battlefield does not contribute to other event points.Event RulesEvent Matching: Lords with castles level 20 or higher can join the matchmaking.Entering the Battlefield: Once the 10 matched lords confirm, they will automatically enter the battlefield.Battle Stage: Capturing buildings will earn a large amount of Battle Points continuously, while collecting Points Supply Boxes will earn a smaller amount of Battle Points.End of Battle: When the battle time ends, the side with the most Battle Points will win.FacilitiesLeague OutpostUnlocks shortly after the battle starts. Boosts your League Hero HP when taken. Grants a moderate amount of points on first capture, and generates points over time.Fairy SpringUnlocks shortly after the battle starts. Boosts your Nature Hero HP when taken. Grants a moderate amount of points on first capture, and generates points over time.Horde FortressUnlocks shortly after the battle starts. Boosts your Horded Hero HP when taken. Grants a moderate amount of points on first capture, and generates points over time.Mana MineAvailable at the start of the battle. Capturing it boosts Hero HP for all heroes. The first capture grants the team a significant score boost, with points continuing to accumulate afterward.RewardsAll players in the battlefield will receive rewards, whether they win or lose. These rewards will be delivered via mail after the battle ends.You'll receive different rewards based on the points you score in the battlefield, with distinct rewards for winner and losers.

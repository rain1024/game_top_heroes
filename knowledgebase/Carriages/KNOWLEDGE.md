# Carriages


## Carriage System

TheCarriage Systemis a key gameplay feature that allows Lords to initiate trades, plunder enemy Carriages, and engage in special activities such as the Treasure Carriage event. Carriages travel random routes, carrying valuable goods that can be plundered by other Lords, and provide various rewards for the player and their guild.

### Overview

- Lords can initiate up to 4 trades daily, each using a Carriage for some time. More unlocked queues mean more Carriages.
- Carriages travel random routes and can be plundered, with no soldier losses in the battles. Each Carriage can be plundered up to 2 times.
- When the Carriage arrives at the destination, the initiator can collect the remaining rewards from the Carriage.
- After 13 days of server opening, the top 20 guilds each day will receive one free Guild Carriage.
- You can purchase 1 extra Carriage attempt daily using Diamonds. Attempts don't roll over and will reset the next day.
- Lords can tag other lords who have looted their Carriage, with a maximum of 5 tags. Tagged lords' Carriages will appear more frequently.

### Trade Rules


#### Initiate Trade

- You can choose a queue to protect the Carriage, each queue can only protect one Carriage at a time.
- When selecting a Carriage, each Carriage has one opportunity to change the reward for free.

#### Rewards

- When the Carriage arrives at the destination, the remaining goods in the Carriage can be collected.
- Using refresh vouchers in succession increases chances of higher rarity Carriages.
- Higher Carriage rarity and castle level mean more valuable basic goods and more advanced items.

#### Plundering Carriages

- When a non-ally Carriage moves in the world, you can plunder it, up to 4 times a day.
- Winning an attack randomly plunders goods and resources from the Carriage.
- A failed plunder does not count towards the plunder count.
- A Carriage that has been plundered twice cannot be plundered again.
- The Carriage cannot be plundered 5 minutes before arriving at the destination.

### Treasure Carriage

- Once your Castle reaches level 15, each time you get a Legendary Carriage, it may turn into a Treasure Carriage.
- Use Premium Carriage Voucher to instantly refresh a Treasure Carriage.
- When you get a Treasure Carriage, you can keep refreshing without lowering its rarity, just changing its rewards.
- You can invite up to 4 members to join and launch the Treasure Carriage.
- Each participant needs to have unused available Carriage Escorts and available Carriage attempts.
- Once the Treasure Carriage sets off, each member will use up one Carriage attempt and earn rewards upon reaching the destination.
- The queue with the highest power at the time of departure will automatically guard this Carriage. Changes to queue formations afterwards won't affect the guard status.
- Each member on board boosts the Treasure Carriage's resource rewards by 10%, shortens travel time by 10%, and increases the chance to reclaim looted items by 25%.
- The Treasure Carriage can depart even if it's not fully staffed.
- The Treasure Carriage can be looted up to three times, using up daily looting attempts.
- You cannot loot Treasure Carriages led by your guild members or your own Carriage.
- There's a 5% chance that a Legendary Carriage will become a Treasure Carriage.

### Hero Adjustments

When adjusting heroes in the queue, the defense queue data will update in real-time. Changes to hero gear, runes, etc., will have a slight delay. For immediate effect, please reboot the game or adjust hero positions.

### Mythical Carriage

TheMythical Carriageis a rare and bountiful carriage that has a small chance of appearing. However, the probability of its appearance will slightly increase after refreshing for 5 times.
- After summoning the Mythical Carriage, you can send an invitation via **Private**, **Guild**, or **World** chat.
- Other players can join the Carriage by clicking on the invitation link.
- The initiator (the one who summoned the Mythical Carriage) has the ability to kick players out of the Carriage.

### Key Features of the Mythical Carriage

- Mythical Carriages offers **more rewards** than the other types of Carriages.
- Once you have summoned the Mythical Carriage, it remains Mythic even if you reroll again, with only the loot changing.
- The Mythical Carriage allows for **4 guild mates** to join and share in the rewards.
- You can **reroll** a certain number of times to try for the best loot without losing the Mythic rarity.
- Mythical Carriages can provide very **valuable rewards**, making them highly coveted.

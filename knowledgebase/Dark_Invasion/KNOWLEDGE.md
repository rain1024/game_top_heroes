# Dark Invasion


## Dark Empire Invasion Event

TheDark Empireevent is primarily a defense event where you have2 chances to failat defending. To achieve the maximum score and rewards, send your Queue to another guild member's castle and ask them to reinforce you. This will allow you to earn points for defending2/3/4 castles, depending on how many Queues you have.

#### How to Maximize Points:

- Every 5th wave targets theDefense Post. Remove your strongest Queue and send it to theDefense Post. Once the wave is completed, return your Queue to the guild member's castle.
- Repeat this process for each wave.
- Keep an eye on your inbox mails: If you receive a failure mail, it’s best to recall your teams back to your castle and defend from there. You can no longer earn points after2 failures. At this point, theDark Invasion Lineswill no longer appear on your castle.

### Rules:

About Permissions:
1. Guild members ranked R4 and abovecan start the event and set the start time.
2. Defending against raiders earns defense points. Reach certain targets to get defense point rewards. Top rankings for both individual and guild defense points will earn extra rewards.
Participation Rules:
1. The guild must have at least20 members.
2. Members must have been in the guild for more than24 hours.

### How to Play:

1. Select the event difficulty in the"Settings"menu.
2. Players with Castle level below 12cannot participate in the event.
3. Reinforcing allies earnsextra defense points(note: it won't affect the ally's points, but the reinforcer gets 50% of the points).
4. Each wave of raiders will target one of the following:Defense PostAll lords' castlesOnline lords' castlesObserve carefully before deciding where to send your troops.
5. Ranking rewardswill be mailed after each event. Leaving the guild midway willforfeit guild ranking rewards.
6. Leaving the guildclears individual points from the original guild but doesn't affect the individual ranking and rewards. Individual ranking points show the highest score of the current period.
7. Battle results will be sent and can be viewed in the report.

### Event Participation Requirements:

- The guild must haveat least 20 membersto participate.
- Lords must have aCastle level of 12 or higher.
- Theguild leaderor anOfficer (R4)can choose the event start time.
- Event time can be adjustedbefore it starts.

### Invasion Waves & Targets:

- The event begins with attacks from theDark Gatetargeting three main objectives:All guild players.Online guild players.TheDefense Post(Fortress).
- There are20 wavesin total. Waves5, 10, 15, and 20specifically target theFortress.
⚠️Important:Losing defense twice means you can no longer score points in the event.

### Optimal Gameplay Strategy:

Castle Defense:
- Do not defend your own castle. Instead, ask your guildmates to defend you.
Distributing Your Queues:
- Send your marches toonline playersafter confirming they will stay online throughout the event.
During Waves 5, 10, 15, and 20:
- Recall the nearest support you've sent to your guildmates and redirect it to theDefense Post(Fortress).
Offline Players:
- Keep a march inside your own castle.
- Send one or more marches to other players or to theDefense Post(one march to the fortress per player is sufficient).
Speed-ups:
- While there might be ways to use speed-ups to gain extra points, it's generallynot worth itfor maximizing rewards.

### Event Difficulty:

Difficulty Levels:
- The event has three difficulty levels. It isextremely easyfor older servers andrelatively easyfor newer servers.
Progression:
- Certain points must be earned to unlock higher difficulty levels.
- It's important to have the most possiblenumber of online playersduring the first attempt at each difficulty level to ensure enough points are recorded for higher difficulty levels in future events.

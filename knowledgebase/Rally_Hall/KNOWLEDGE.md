# Rally Hall

Rally HallTheRally Hallis a vital building that increases the soldier capacity for your rally queue, allowing more troops to join rallies and enhancing the overall effectiveness of your rallies. Upgrading the Rally Hall not only boosts the maximum troop limit but also increases the number of rally members, enabling larger coordinated attacks.Key FeaturesIncreased Troop Limit: Each upgrade of the Rally Hall raises the maximum number of troops that can be included in a rally, helping your army grow stronger and more formidable in battle.Rally Members: The number of members that can join your rally is fixed at four until the Rally Hall reaches level 30, at which point the number of available rally members increases to 5. This expansion enables larger and more impactful rallies.World Map Damage: As the Rally Hall is upgraded, your rally’s damage on the world map improves, making your army more capable of dealing with various threats in the world. Additionally, damage reduction increases as the building level rises, improving your rally's defense.Level ProgressionWith each level, the Rally Hall provides greater benefits:Level 0starts with a base troop limit of 12,000 and four members in the rally.Level 40offers the maximum capacity of 36,000 troops and six rally members, as well as the highest possible World Map Rally Damage (10%) and World Map Rally Damage Reduction (10%).Strategy BenefitsThe Rally Hall plays a crucial role in rally-based strategies, allowing you to summon and coordinate with more players to launch effective attacks. Higher-level rallies will help your alliance dominate on the world map, whether you're taking on powerful bosses, capturing strongholds, or defeating enemy troops.

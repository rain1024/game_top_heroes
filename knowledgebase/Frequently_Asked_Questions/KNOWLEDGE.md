# Frequently Asked Questions


## How should I spend my diamonds?

It's recommended to save up diamonds as much as you can. Most players use them to speed-up lengthy build times. They're also good in a pinch if you're being attacked and out of advanced relocates, so you can teleport to safety for 2k diamonds. Many players also use them to buy things like meat, speed-ups, and gear enhance runes in the store.
Do not use diamonds on recruitment tickets in the Tavern, as the Tavern honestly sucks. Only use them to purchase 10 legendary shards for 1k diamonds if you reach VIP8+.

## What should I spend money on?

If you are to spend money, it's recommended that you buy the "BEST PRICE" and "Adventure Begins" packs. They are cheap and give good rewards.
You can buy hero shards when their packs arrive, but you should keep in mind that most heroes can be obtained for free in the Tavern, and on top of that, most heroes will become obsolete once Mythics drop in your server (around the beginning of month 3 on your server). So think about your endgame line-up before you purchase a pack. It's actually cheaper to buy a Mythic level hero once and then use universal legendary hero shards on it, as Legendary heroes require you to buy multiple Soul Stones to awaken and reach Mythic status, meaning Legendary heroes are significantly more expensive in the long run.

# Storm Maiden

Storm Maiden is one of the first mythic Horde heroes obtained in the game. She specializes in damage dealing, sustained combat, and multi-target AoE damage dealing.
[[File:|style="border-radius: 30px;"]]Storm MaidenRacer Class:Horde

## Skills


### Whirlwind Feathers

Description:Unleashes 2 feather projectiles per second to randomly attack enemies within range, dealing [x]% attack damage for 3 seconds. Cooldown: 8 seconds. This skill can be used while performing basic attacks.
- ★ Increase skill damage by 17.5%, dealing additional damage equal to 2% of the target's current HP each time (up to a maximum of 100% of Storm Maiden's Attack).
- ★★ Skill damage increases by 17.5%.
- ★★★ Increases skill damage by 17.5%, and increases attack speed by 100% upon activation.

### Lightning Storm

Description:Ultimate Skill: Releases a lightning-infused tornado that moves slowly, dealing damage equal to [x]% of the Attack to enemies in its path and knocking them into the air. Cooldown: 22 seconds.
- ★ Skill damage increases by 55%.
- ★★ Skill damage increases by 55%.
- ★★★ Increases skill damage by 55%. After using this skill, Storm Maiden's normal attack turns into a Chain Lightning that jumps to 3 targets, lasting 6 seconds.

### Wings of Freedom

Description:Grants [x]% dodge rate to this hero, and additionally adds the same effect for the entire queue when deployed, this hero also gains this bonus.
- ★ Increase the effect bonus by 1%.
- ★★ Increase the effect bonus by 1%.
- ★★★ Increase the effect bonus by 1%, granting 10% increased damage to this hero, not shared with deployed heroes.

### Tornado

Description:Channels a whirlwind for 2 seconds, immune to bullet damage during this time, then releases a tornado dealing [x]% attack damage every 0.5 seconds, gradually expanding the attack range, lasting 2.5 seconds. Cooldown: 12 seconds.
- ★ Skill damage increases by 22.5%.
- ★★ Skill damage increases by 22.5%, reduces target's healing received by 25%, lasting 5 seconds.
- ★★★ Skill damage increases by 22.5%.

### Wind Field

Description:Each use of Tornado increases the Queue's attack by [x]%, and reduces the Tornado's cooldown by 1 second, disappearing after stacking 5 times.
- ★ Increases the effect bonus by 1%.
- ★★ Increases the effect bonus by 1%.
- ★★★ Boosts effect by 1%, stackable up to 7 times before disappearing.

## Gear Skills


### Stormbringer

Exclusive Skill: Storm Mastery. When attacking, apply 1 stacks of Static Charge to the target. Each stack increases damage taken from Storm Maiden by 4%, lasting 8 seconds. The effect can stack up to 10 times, and the duration of the Static Charge does not reset with additional stacks.
- Lv. 5: This hero's Skill Critical Damage increased by 15%.
- Lv. 10: Each attack adds 1 additional stacks of Static Charge, allowing the Storm Maiden to penetrate shields on marked targets.
- Lv. 15: At the beginning of the battle, boost all your Horde heroes' Attack by 10%.
- Lv. 20: Static Charge duration is shortened by 2 seconds and detonates upon expiration, dealing area damage. Each stack explodes and deals damage equal to 30% of Storm Maiden's Attack. The explosion is also triggered if the target dies.

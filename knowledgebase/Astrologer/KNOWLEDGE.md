# Astrologer

The Astrologer is a Legendary category hero in the league faction, specializing in damage dealing, burst damage, and single-target damage.
Shards unlock in the Tavern at recruitment Lv. 10
AstrologerRacer Class:League

## Skills


### Ray Beam

Summon beams to inflict xx% damage to a small group of enemies.  This skill has a 20% increased Critical Hit Rate.  Cooldown: 10 seconds.

### Foresight

Increase the Critical Rate by xx% and this hero can move while performing normal attacks.

### Insights

Increase the Critical Damage.  Gain 1% Critical Damage per 2.5% Critical Rate, up to xx%

### Solar Beam

Ultimate Skill: Summon 15 beam strikes within 6 seconds, dealing damage to enemies in the area.  Each beam strike deals damage to a single target, and the damage increases by 5% with each subsequent hit on the same target.  This skill has a 10% increased Critical Hit Rate.  Cooldown: 21 seconds.  Prioritizes attacking Hero characters.

### Arc Light

Release a laser that deals xx% damage to enemies in its path.  Each hit increases the damage by 5%, stacking up to 5 stacks for 10 seconds.  Cooldown: 15 seconds.

### Rune Upgrade System

The Rune Upgrade System allows players to upgrade runes of various levels to higher levels by using multiple runes of the same level. Each upgrade requires three runes of a given level to form one rune of the next level. The upgrade process follows a recursive pattern, with each level requiring an exponentially increasing number of lower-level runes. This system plays a key role in enhancing player progression, allowing for the creation of powerful high-level runes.

#### Rune Levels and Costs

In the Rune Upgrade System, there are 10 levels of runes, from Level 1 to Level 10. To upgrade a rune to the next level, the following costs in runes of the lower level are required:
- Level 1 Rune:The basic starting point. No upgrades are required for a Level 1 rune.
- Level 2 Rune:Requires 3 Level 1 runes.Cost to Upgrade to Level 2: 3 Level 1 runes.
- Level 3 Rune:Requires 3 Level 2 runes. Since 3 Level 2 runes are needed, and each Level 2 rune requires 3 Level 1 runes to craft, the total cost to upgrade to a Level 3 rune is:Cost to Upgrade to Level 3: 9 Level 1 runes.
- Level 4 Rune:Requires 3 Level 3 runes, which in turn require 9 Level 1 runes each. Therefore, to upgrade to a Level 4 rune:Cost to Upgrade to Level 4: 27 Level 1 runes.
- Level 5 Rune:Requires 3 Level 4 runes. With each Level 4 rune costing 27 Level 1 runes, the cost to upgrade to Level 5 is:Cost to Upgrade to Level 5: 81 Level 1 runes.
- Level 6 Rune:Requires 3 Level 5 runes. With each Level 5 rune requiring 81 Level 1 runes:Cost to Upgrade to Level 6: 243 Level 1 runes.
- Level 7 Rune:Requires 3 Level 6 runes, and each Level 6 rune requires 243 Level 1 runes:Cost to Upgrade to Level 7: 729 Level 1 runes.
- Level 8 Rune:Requires 3 Level 7 runes. Each Level 7 rune requires 729 Level 1 runes:Cost to Upgrade to Level 8: 2,187 Level 1 runes.
- Level 9 Rune:Requires 3 Level 8 runes, and each Level 8 rune costs 2,187 Level 1 runes:Cost to Upgrade to Level 9: 6,561 Level 1 runes.
- Level 10 Rune:The highest rune level. Requires 3 Level 9 runes, with each Level 9 rune requiring 6,561 Level 1 runes:Cost to Upgrade to Level 10: 19,683 Level 1 runes.

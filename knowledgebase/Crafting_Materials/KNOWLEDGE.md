# Crafting Materials


## Raw Ore

Raw Ore is used for crafting gear. It can be obtained through a Raw Ore Mine, dismantling gear at the Blacksmith, or disassembling Bronze Ore (1 Bronze Ore disassembles into 4 Raw Ore).

## Bronze Ore

Bronze Ore is used for crafting gear. It can be obtained through dismantling gear at the Blacksmith, crafting at the Blacksmith (1 Bronze Ore requires 4 Raw Ore),  or disassembling Fine Iron Ingots (1 Fine Iron Ingot disassembles into 4 Bronze Ore).

## Fine Iron Ingot

Fine Iron Ingots are used for crafting gear. It can be obtained through dismantling gear at the Blacksmith, crafting at the Blacksmith (1 Fine Iron Ingot requires 4 Bronze Ore), or disassembling Amethyst Plates (1 Amethyst Plate disassembles into 4 Fine Iron Ingots).

## Amethyst Plate

Amethyst Plates are used for crafting gear. It can be obtained through dismantling gear at the Blacksmith, crafting at the Blacksmith (1 Amethyst Plate requires 4 Fine Iron Ingots), or disassembling Solar Gold Ingots (1 Solar Gold Ingot disassembles into 4 Amethyst Plates).

## Solar Gold Ingot

Amethyst Plates are used for crafting gear. It can be obtained through dismantling gear at the Blacksmith, or crafting at the Blacksmith (1 Solar Gold Ingot requires 4 Amethyst Plates).

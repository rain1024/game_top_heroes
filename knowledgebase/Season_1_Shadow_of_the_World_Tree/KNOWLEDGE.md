# Season 1 Shadow of the World Tree

Wasteland CrisisStoryThe World Tree, the source of life and cradle of all creatures, has stood in the heart of the primeval forest since ancient times, filling the land with life.The first beings, playful pixies, emerged from plants and, using the tree's life force, shaped early creatures and marvels of nature. But over time, their strength waned, and they returned to the World Tree, falling into a deep sleep.Meanwhile, new civilizations flourished, building kingdoms and grand empires across the land. But then the Dark Empire arose. Dark mages wielding black magic destroyed cities, enslaved people, and transformed them into obedient soldiers, forming a relentless Dark Legion. Yet even that wasn't enough - the mages wanted the power of the World Tree to rule the world.Recently, in a great clash, the Dark Empire unleashed a deadly serpent that drained the World Tree's life energy, leaving the Allied Vanguard weakened. Without this life force, the forest is turning to desert, and dark creatures are awakening. The world is on the edge of collapse!You, my Lord, are our final hope.

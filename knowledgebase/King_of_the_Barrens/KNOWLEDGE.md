# King of the Barrens

King of the BarrensThe King of the Barrens is a formidable elemental lord who wanders the desert map unpredictably, a force of nature that demands respect. With immense strength, this powerful being presents a severe challenge to any who dare engage. Be warned—failing to defeat the King of the Barrens will trigger a devastating counterattack on the castle of the rally initiator, with disastrous consequences.At levels 1 through 3, the King of the Barrens is as powerful as 6 through 8 Legion Bosses, making it a daunting adversary. Unlike Legion bosses, however, the King of the Barrens requires careful preparation, as attacking it comes at the cost of valuable soldiers. You only have three attempts per day to face this behemoth, so be strategic in your approach.

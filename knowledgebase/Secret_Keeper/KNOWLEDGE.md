# Secret Keeper

The Secret Keeper is a Legendary category Tank hero in the League faction, specializing in tanking, controlling enemies, and buffs.
He is obtainable in the Tavern at Recruitment Level 3 and is the first Legendary Hero available to the player.

### Absolute Zero

Exclusive Skill (Frozen Barrier)
Every 12 seconds, a shield is granted to 3 random heroes, equal to 180% of Secret Keeper's attack, lasting 10 seconds, absorbing 50% of damage taken.

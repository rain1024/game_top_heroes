Wizardis a classic backline magic damage dealer inTop Heroes. Known for his high burst potential and crowd control, he excels in early-to-mid battles where immediate impact is critical. His spells bring both raw firepower and tactical utility to any team composition.

## Lore

Once a wandering scholar of the arcane, the Wizard now serves as a protector of the realm’s balance. With his enchanted staff, ancient spellbook, and flowing blue robes, he commands lightning and fire with a mere flick of his hand. Though small in stature, his presence on the battlefield is anything but.

## Skills

1. Arcane Blast (Ultimate) 🌩️
Unleashes a massive arcane bolt that hits all enemies, with a high chance to stun.
→ Great for disabling enemy backlines before they can act.
2. Firebolt 🔥
Deals high single-target fire damage. Has a small burn-over-time effect.
→ Effective against frontline tanks or healers.
3. Mana Shield 🔷
Grants the Wizard a protective barrier that absorbs damage based on INT.
→ Offers survivability in PvP where sniping is common.
4. Knowledge of the Ages (Passive) 📘
Each turn increases his INT by 3% (stacks up to 5 times).
→ Makes him more dangerous the longer he survives.

# Lord's Hall

Lord Traits SystemThe Lord Traits System is a critical gameplay feature that allows players to upgrade and unlock Lord Traits. These traits provide important buffs and abilities to enhance the Lord's strength, construction capabilities, and combat effectiveness.After unlocking the Lord's Hall, Lords can choose between two paths: the War Lord or the Harvest Lord, each with their own strengths and advantages.Lord's Traits CategoriesHarvest Lord's TraitsWar Lord's TraitsLord's HallBoth Trait paths are accessible through the passive trees in the Lord's Hall, a building unlocked at Castle level 25.Glory MedalsGlory Medals are a key currency used to upgrade the Lord's Hall, providing Lord Points with each level gained.Glory Medals are earned exclusively through participation in the Guild Arms Race event. This event is the only way to acquire Glory Medals, which are required to advance in the Lord's Hall upgrade process.The Guild Arms Race event is held monthly, depending on the schedule, so players must participate during the event window to earn these medals.Lord's PointsLord's Points are essential for acquiring and leveling up Lord's Traits and can be earned by upgrading the Lord's Hall.Lord's Traits SelectionYou can choose between two paths: Harvest Lord and War Lord. Here's a quick breakdown to help you decide.Harvest Lord! Why & When?The Harvest Lord is an excellent choice for players focused on construction buffs. It significantly accelerates the journey to Castle and Barracks level 40.Ideal for free-to-play players or those who prefer a peaceful early-game experience. It supports your fighters with useful tools such as Dark & Light Towers, which can be constructed without engaging in combat.War Lord! Why & When?The War Lord is a better choice for players invested in PVP and combat-heavy gameplay.War Lord is more beneficial once players reach Castle level 40 or when they need increased combat power.This option is perfect for players who spend regularly and are committed to being competitive in both early and end-game content.You can switch from Harvest Lord to War Lord using a Trait Reset Scroll, available for purchase in the Daily Diamond Shop, or take advantage of the free weekly reset.Harvest Lord's Notable TraitsIncrease Construction SpeedNote: Up to 40% boost to construction speed.Load Capacity IncreaseNote: Loot more resources per successful attack or while gathering.Gathering Reward PackNote: Earn 10x5 rewards daily, totaling 50 (similar to a silver chest).Guild Arms World Map PointsNote: Helps score more points during the first stage of Arms Day.Auto Gathering FacilityNote: Build an Auto Gathering Facility on the world map to gather resources. You can recall your troops after the facility is built.Tower of LightNote: Can be built on the world map (up to 2 max) to boost friendly queues, increasing damage dealt and damage reduced by 15% at max level.Tower of DarknessNote: Can debuff enemy players within range, reducing damage dealt and damage reduced by 16% at max level.Super RallyNote: Increases the capacity of soldiers that can participate in a rally.Rally HornNote: Increases the HP of rally participants' heroes by up to 50%. The effect can stack with other players' Rally Horns.War Lord's Notable TraitsIncrease Attack During Castle Invasion by 15%Note: Boosts your heroes' attack when attacking castles in the open world.Increase Wounded Soldier Healing Speed by 30%Note: Speeds up the recovery of troops wounded in battle.Increase League Soldier HP by 60%Note: Increases the durability of League soldiers in battle.Increase Horde Soldier HP by 60%Note: Increases the durability of Horde soldiers in battle.Increase Nature Soldier HP by 60%Note: Increases the durability of Nature soldiers in battle.Increase League Soldiers' Attack by 30%Note: Improves offensive strength of League troops.Increase Horde Soldiers' Attack by 30%Note: Improves offensive strength of Horde troops.Increase Nature Soldiers' Attack by 30%Note: Improves offensive strength of Nature troops.Increase League March Size by +100Note: Expands League heroes' march size, adding 10 troops per level, up to 100 at max level.Increase Horde March Size by +100Note: Expands Horde heroes' march size, adding 10 troops per level, up to 100 at max level.Increase Nature March Size by +100Note: Expands Nature heroes' march size, adding 10 troops per level, up to 100 at max level.Increase March Speed When Assisting Allies by 30%Note: Enhances the speed of march queues when helping allies in combat.Increase Attack by 30% When Assisting AlliesNote: Boosts the offensive power of support heroes during collaborative battles.Increase HP by 30% When Assisting AlliesNote: Enhances the survivability of heroes who assist allies in battle.Consecutive Battle Limit Up to 5Note: Expands the number of battles your Queue can engage in consecutively, allowing you to push through reinforcements.

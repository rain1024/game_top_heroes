## Awakening


#### Tier 1: Lv. 1 Sacred Order

Awakening Passive Skill. At the start of the battle, all allies' Attack and HP increase by 10%, League Heroes gain a shield equal to 5% of their maximum HP.

#### Tier 2: Shield of Guardians

Awakening Skill Enhancement. Power up Shield of Faith. The shield calls Dawn Guardians for battle. When the shield expires, the Guardians impact a large enemy area, stunning targets for 1-3s. Longer shield duration equals longer stun.

#### Tier 3: Lv. 2 Sacred Order

Awakening Passive Skill. At the start of battle, all allies' Attack and HP increase by 20%, League Heroes gain a shield equal to 10% of their maximum HP.

#### Tier 4: Awakening: Adjudicator

Unlock Adjudicator's Awakening Skin. Improve hero rarity. Greatly increase attributes and troop limit.

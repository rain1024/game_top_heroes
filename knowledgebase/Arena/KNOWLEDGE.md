# Arena


## How to Play

The Ranked Match is a competitive event where lords battle to climb the ranks and earn rewards.

## Challenge Mechanics

1. Tap the Challenge button to battle other lords and earn points. Higher ranks grant better rewards.
2. Everyone starts with 1,000 points. Winners gain points; losers lose points.
3. Use the Formation button to adjust your hero lineup.
4. In Ranked Matches, battles use a full march with no injuries or deaths.

## Challenge Limits

- Each player gets 5 free challenges daily.
- Unused challenges can be stored, up to a maximum of 15.

## Ranked Match Duration

- The event lasts 7 days.
- The final hour is for result finalization, and no challenges can be made during this time.
- Rewards are sent via mail once rankings are settled.

## Defensive Power

- Your defensive power is used for ranked battles.
- Defensive power refreshes upon re-login.

#### Tier 1: Burst Rune

Awakening Passive Skill. When deployed, all your heroes' skill damage increases by 8%.

Awakening Skill Enhancement. Enhance Meteor Blaze, increase skill release speed, deal 150% extra damage to summoned units. After release, apply 3 stacks of burning effect to all hit targets.

Awakening Passive Skill. When deployed, all your heroes' skill damage increases by 14%.

Unlock Pyromancer's Awakening Skin. Improve hero rarity. Greatly increase attributes and troop limit.

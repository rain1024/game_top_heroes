# Guild Race

Event OverviewThis event allows Guilds to compete for ranking rewards by completing various tasks. Players can earn Guild Honor, which can be exchanged for rare items. Guilds must meet specific entry requirements, and strategic participation is key to achieving the best rewards.Guild Entry RequirementsTo participate, a Guild must meet the following conditions:The Guild must have at least 10 members at the start of the event.At least 10 members must be active when the event begins.Guilds cannot join mid-event, even if they later meet the conditions.Personal Entry RequirementsIndividual players must also meet specific requirements:Your Castle must be at least level 12 to participate.If you leave a Guild, you cannot rejoin the event unless you join a qualifying Guild.After switching Guilds, you can still participate, but you will not earn points for the new Guild or qualify for Guild ranking rewards.Event MatchingAt the start of the event, 4 Guilds of similar tier will be grouped together.These matched Guilds will compete in both individual and Guild rankings.Rank PromotionAll Guilds start at the base rank in their first event.At the end of the event:* The top Guild is promoted to a higher rank.
 * The bottom two Guilds are demoted to a lower rank.Task SystemTasks are divided into individual tasks and public tasks (Guild-wide).Task TypesTask TypeDescriptionIndividual TasksOnly visible to the player.Public TasksVisible to all Guild members. Once taken, it cannot be taken by another member until completed or abandoned.Task LimitsLords can only have one active task at a time.Abandoning TasksActive tasks can be abandoned, resetting the task and returning the attempt.Task CountEvery Lord starts with 9 task attempts at the beginning of the event.Additional attempts can be purchased daily.Unused attempts will expire at the end of the event.Task RefreshLords can manually refresh personal tasks (10-minute cooldown).Diamonds can be used to refresh immediately.Guild Masters & R4 members can refresh all public tasks (120-minute cooldown).All tasks automatically refresh daily if unclaimed.Event RewardsCompleting tasks earns Guild Honor and Matching Points.

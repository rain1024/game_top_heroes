# Pets

Pet Paradise and Egg HatchingOnce you reach **Castle Level 25**, you’ll unlock the **Pet Paradise** and **Pet Food Factory** buildings, but only when the Pet system has been introduced to your server. If you're at Castle 25 and don't see the Pet Paradise building or eggs in the Guild Point store, it means pets are not yet available on your server.Building the Pet ParadiseTo begin, you need to build **Pet Paradise** at Castle Level 25. Once it’s constructed, you’ll gain access to **Pet Bounties** and **Pet Egg Hatching**.- **Pet Paradise** allows you to hatch eggs collected through bounties and store pet food.
- You can complete **3 free bounties daily**, with an additional bounty available for **1,000 gems**.
- The bounties have a difficulty level comparable to **Legion Boss 6**, and each bounty may spawn a **Slime Boss** (a Pet Monster).Obtaining Pet EggsEggs are collected by completing Pet Bounties. These bounties can yield eggs, with a chance for a pet to hatch once you place them in the Pet Paradise.- **Slime Bosses** drop eggs (with a guaranteed pet food reward).
- There is a **33.3% chance** for a **Level 1 Slime Boss** to drop an egg, and a slightly higher chance from **Level 2** and higher Pet Monsters (available at Pet Paradise Level 35+).Once you’ve collected eggs, they must be incubated in the **Pet Paradise**. The incubation process takes several hours before the egg hatches.Hatching and Rarity of EggsEggs come in two colors, each with different chances of hatching a rare pet:Blue Egg- **20% chance** to hatch a **Blue (Rare)** pet.
- If unsuccessful, a **Green (Common)** Slime pet will hatch.Green Egg- **10% chance** to hatch a **Blue (Rare)** pet.
- If unsuccessful, a **Green (Common)** Slime pet will hatch.Pet Growth and UpgradesOnce you hatch your pets, you can level them up and promote them to improve their abilities.

# Guild Sacred Spring

Guild Sacred SpringThe Guild Sacred Spring provides significant buffs to members within its range!  
It increases the water supply for guild members, with higher levels providing greater boosts.Leveling Up the Sacred Spring- Use Golden Roots to raise the Sacred Spring’s level.  
- As the guild collects more Golden Roots, the Sacred Spring will automatically level up.  
- The more Golden Roots the guild has, the higher the level of the Sacred Spring.Golden RootsGolden Roots are used to upgrade the Guild Sacred Spring.

# Ancient Battlefield

This is the version of the Ancient Battlefield event that is Guild vs. Guild.
The Ancient Battlefield is an event in which two guilds from any server are paired together to claim facilities and gain the most points over the opposing team.
Points gained in this event are Combat Valor, which are redeemed in the points store.

## Story

This event tests not just strength but also wisdom and strategy. Lords must carefully choose and pair their heroes, overcome challenges, defeat strong enemies, and finally win the treasures of the Ancient Battlefield. All these will become part of their legendary tales on this fantasy land.

## How to Play

Guilds meeting the criteria can sign up for the Ancient Battlefield. R4 or R5 members select the battle time and participants during signup.
Conditions to enter the battlefield are:
1. The Lord is a main or backup member for this battle.
2. The Lord's castle is on their home server.
During the battle stage, eligible Lords can enter the battlefield. Upon entering, participants from both sides go to a safe zone (spawn area) where they can't be attacked.
1. Two Guilds are matched to the same battlefield. Capture as many buildings as possible for points. Winning in the Brawl Arena is also key.
2. The Guild that first captures or holds buildings gets lots of "Points", with the highest-scoring side winning.
3. In the Ancient Battlefield, soldiers are given based on your barracks and training ground levels, and they won't die.
4. In battle, you'll get a free teleport chance at regular intervals.
5. Each guild can send up to 20 Lords to the Ancient Battlefield.
6. Killing or healing soldiers in the battlefield will not count towards points in other events.

## Event Stages


#### Registration Stage

Lords send their strongest troops to the Ancient Battlefield without using a real march queue. After signup, lords can still use that troop to attack and capture facilities.

#### Battle Stage

No more signups or queue changes once this stage starts. Random battles with members of the opposing guild occur in the arena.
1. Stage 1: League Outpost, Fairy Spring, Horde Stronghold, and Mana Mine become capturable and Lords can teleport near these facilities.
2. Stage 2: Champion's Sword and Champion's Shield become capturable.
3. Stage 3: Ancient Arena opens, a chance to widen or close the points gap.

#### Settlement Stage

Winning guild members get lots of Points after the battle. The side with more points when the battlefield countdown ends wins.

## Facilities


### League Outpost

This facility is available from the start of the battle, capturing it speeds up the time it takes for members to take over facilities, and the effect is cumulative. It also provides some points. Taking this facility helps you set up your strategy faster.

### Fairy Spring

This facility is available from the start of the battle, capturing it greatly reduces the time needed to heal soldiers, and the effect is cumulative. It also provides some points. If you have heavy troop losses, prioritize taking the Fairy Spring.

### Horde Fortress

This facility is available from the start of the battle. Capturing it shortens the cooldown for free relocation, and the effect is cumulative. If your strategy is highly mobile, taking the Horde Fortress is crucial.

### Mana Mine

This facility is available from the start of the battle, capturing it gives many Points. It's the primary source of points and a key strategic location.

### Champion's Sword

This facility opens some time after the battle starts, capturing it boosts the hero attributes of all your members. It also grants some points.

### Champion's Shield

This facility opens some time after the battle starts, capturing it boosts the hero attributes of all your members. It also grants some points.

### Brawl Arena

The Brawl Arena opens some time after the battle begins. Lords can send troops to join (it doesn't use up your real troop queue). Once the Brawl Arena starts, signed-up teams will be randomly matched for fights. Winning Lords and Guilds earn lots of points.

## Rewards


#### All-Member Rewards

Rewards for all members of the Guild, divided into Winner's Rewards and Loser's Rewards. After the battle in the Ancient Battlefield ends, they are distributed to all members of the Guild via mail.*Members who join after registration will not be eligible for rewards.

#### Participation Rewards

All members who score >2000 in the battlefield can receive them, divided into Winner's Rewards and Loser's Rewards. After the battle in the Ancient Battlefield ends, they are distributed to all members of the Guild via mail.

#### Progress Rewards

Earn different reward tiers based on the score from the Ancient Battlefield. Split into Winner's Rewards and Loser's Rewards.

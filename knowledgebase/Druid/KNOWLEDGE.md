# Druid

The Druid is the Legendary category healer in the Nature faction, specializing in healing, buffs, and summoning.
Shards unlock in the Tavern at recruitment Lv. 11
[[File:|style="border-radius: 30px;"]]Racer Class:{{{class}}}

## Skills


### Nature's Blessing

Healing a single character in your queue, allowing it to recover HP equal to [x]% of the Attack over 3 seconds. Cooldown: 10 seconds.

### Force of Nature

Nature's Blessing grants the target a shield equal to [x]% of the Attack. 40% of damage taken can be absorbed each time, with a duration of 5 seconds.

### Natural Affinity

Nature's Call boosts Treeman HP by an extra [x]%.

### Healing Rain

Ultimate Skill: Summon Rain of Healing, restoring HP equal to [x]% of Druid's Attack to allies within range. Cooldown: 21 seconds.

### Nature's Call

Summon 2 Tiny Treemen allies to assist in battle. The Treemen inherit [x]% of the Druid's current Attack and HP. The Treemen last for 12 seconds and have a cooldown of 15 seconds.

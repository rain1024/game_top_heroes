# Guild Carriage


## Guild Carriage System

The Guild Carriage system allows players to participate in a shared event where rewards are earned based on selection and defense of a carriage. The system involves selecting a Guild Carriage Captain and joining different carts to receive rewards. Here's how it works:

### Selecting the Guild Carriage Captain

- R4/R5 members select the Guild Carriage Captain, who is responsible for defending the carriage.
- The carriage consists of 5 carts: 1 for the leader (Captain) and 4 for random guildmates.
- 5 people will receive rewards in total. The Guild Captain and 4 other guildmates will be selected for carts.

### Choosing Carts

- Guild members who are not selected as the captain can choose a cart they want to join.
- Once the carriage departs, one person will be randomly chosen to join each cart (total of 5 people per event).
- If multiple guild members select the same cart, it will be determined by luck who gets to join.
- After 4 refreshes of the guild carriage (costing 5 tickets each), the chance for legendary carriages becomes almost guaranteed.

### Plundering and Defending

- Plundering occurs if the carriage is attacked. When you plunder, you only fight the Guild Carriage Captain, not the entire carriage.
- If your server experiences frequent plunder, it’s crucial to choose a strong captain to protect rewards.
- If your server is peaceful, it might not matter who is selected as the captain.

### Carriage Guarding and Rewards

- When the carriage sets off, the queue with the highest power will automatically guard it.
- No changes to the queue formations after departure will affect the guard status.
- Each member onboard boosts the Treasure Carriage’s resource rewards by 10%, shortens travel time by 10%, and increases the chance to reclaim plundered items by 25%.
- Reclaiming Items: If the carriage is plundered, there is a chance to reclaim one lost item.
- The Treasure Carriage can be plundered up to three times, which counts towards the daily plunder limit.

### How To Play:

1. You can invite up to 4 players to join and set off the Treasure Carriage. 2. Each participant needs to have an available Carriage guard queue and Carriage attempts. 3. Once the Treasure Carriage sets off, each member will use one Carriage attempt and earn rewards upon reaching the destination. 4. The queue with the highest power at the time of departure will automatically guard this Carriage. Changes to queue formations afterwards won't affect the guard status. 5. Each member onboard boosts the Treasure Carriage's resource rewards by 10%, shortens travel time by 10%, and increases the chance to reclaim items plundered by 25%. 6. The Treasure Carriage can depart even if it's not fully staffed. 7. The Treasure Carriage can be plundered up to three times, which will be counted in the daily plunder count. 8. You cannot plunder Treasure Carriages guarded by your guild members or your own Carriage.

### Cargo Selection

- After the Guild Carriage starts, choose the cargo you prefer.
- After a 4-hour waiting period, one lucky member will be randomly assigned to each cargo.

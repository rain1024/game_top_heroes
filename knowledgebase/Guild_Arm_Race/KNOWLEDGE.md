# Guild Arm Race


## How to Play

The Arms Race is a six-day Guild event where Guilds compete in various military-themed activities. Completing daily tasks earns points, and rewards are distributed based on rankings.

## Arms Race Matchmaking

To resist the powerful invasion of the Dark Empire, various Guilds have been called upon by the kingdom to actively engage in military activities.
1. Every two Guilds are randomly matched up as a group to compete.
2. A Guild's participation is determined by its activity level and total power.
3. Some Guilds may be excluded due to insufficient activity.

## Arms Race Score

- The event lasts for six days, with a different theme each day.
- Completing themed tasks earns points toward the Guild’s total.
- If a player leaves a Guild, only their daily points are inherited; cumulative points are lost.
- Returning to a Guild does not restore previous points, and individual ranking points are reset.
- Points cannot be earned in the final 15 minutes of each day.
- The Arms Race officially begins when the event announcement appears. Changing Guilds during the event prevents point contribution to a new Guild.

## Themes and Victory Points


| Day | Theme | Winning Side Points | Earning Points |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day 1 | Territory Building | 2* | Getting power from upgrading buildings, using speed-ups for building construction, buying diamonds. |
| Day 2 | Tech Boost | 2 | Getting power from researching tech, using speed-ups for tech research, buying diamonds. |
| Day 3 | Global Battles | 2 | Defeating monsters and bosses on World Map, buying diamonds. |
| Day 4 | Call of the Hero | 2 | Recruiting heroes in the Tavern, consuming Hero Shards, buying diamonds. |
| Day 5 | Combat Ready | 2 | Training solders, using speed-ups on soldier training, buying diamonds. |
| Day 6 | Invincible Warrior | 4 | Killing enemy soldiers, healing soldiers, buying diamonds. |

*The first Guild Arms Race only gives one point for the first day, subsequent Arms Races yield 2 points for the first day.

## Arms Race Results

- The daily Guild points are the combined total of all members' earned points.
- The Guild with the most points each day wins and earns Victory Points.
- The losing Guild still earns Loser Points.
- The final winner is determined by cumulative points over six days, not daily quest points.
- If both Guilds have the same victory points, the winner is decided by total event points.

## Arms Race Rewards


| Reward Type | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Individual Rewards | Earned by completing daily quests and reaching point milestones. |
| Technology Milestone Rewards | Additional bonuses for reaching tech progression goals. |
| Once More Rewards | Unclaimed rewards can be re-earned but cannot be obtained twice if already claimed. |
| Daily Settlement Rewards | Points are settled daily, and rewards vary based on victory or defeat. |
| Final Guild Rewards | Based on the final ranking of the Guild after six days. |
| Ranking Rewards | Given based on individual performance within the Guild. |


## Reward Distribution

1. Individual progress points must be claimed manually.
2. Daily points and weekly victory point rewards are sent via mail.
3. Winning and losing sides receive different rewards based on performance.

## Strategies

- Save up on speed-ups, Hero Shards, recruitment tokens, and stamina before the race.
- Level up buildings and research tech without collecting them when they are done until the round that requires them begins. Also save buildings and research that can be completely quicky for the race.
- Invincible Warrior:Guilds other than one's opponent in the race can collaborate to attack each other mutually to farm points from killing and injuring similarly leveled soldiers.Attacking low-level castles yields very few points, as they tend to have fewer soldiers and they are not leveled very high, so it is not recommended and will only generate animosity if done without an agreement.
- Invincible Warrior (pacifist):Soldiers can be killed and injured en masse by attacking high level bosses, Resource Guards, and Resource Tiles, meaning injured soldiers can be farmed to heal for points without having to make any attacks on castles or being attacked. This will greatly decrease power while soldiers are in the hospital, so it is recommended to use a shield at all times if using this strategy. This is also very slow and consumes a great deal of resources, so it is recommended only to pacifists and low-level castles.

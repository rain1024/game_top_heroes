# Fort Contest

The Fort Contest is a Guild Vs. Guild event where two guilds are matched together to attack and defend forts in a designated event screen.Prep StageEach fort can hold up to 20 queues. Every guild member can participate in placing their queues into forts.Prep stage lasts three days.Battle StageOnly members that are top 20 (in later events, it can go down to top 10) in power have the ability to attack the enemy forts when the battle stage begins. Each of the top 20 members get to attack up to four times, two times per garrison queue, then get 1 more attack per queue every four hours.To attack forts in the midline, at least one of the frontline has to reach below 50% capacity, and for the back castle to be hit, at least one of the forts in the midline has to reach below 50% capacity.Battle stage lasts one day.RewardsSet up a queue and select any fort to defend to receive a preparation reward.Players who attack an enemy fort once during the battle stage will receive a combat reward for every attack.All members of the winning guild will receive a victory reward.All members of the losing guild will receive a defeat reward (half of victory reward).StrategiesThe only strategy for this event is to have active guild members that use their attacks every four hours, placement of high and low power queues is mostly insignificant.

# Bishop

[[File:|style="border-radius: 30px;"]]Racer Class:{{{class}}}

## Skills


### Fair Strike

Summon a Sword of Justice to strike 3 random enemies, dealing % damage equals to the attack and inflicting an additional 200% damage to summoned units. Cooldown: 8 seconds.
- * Increase skill damage by 25%. Fair Strike's cooldown is reduced by 50% when it kills any target. (Requires 1 gold star)
- ** Skill damage increases by 25% (Requires 1 red star)
- *** Increase skill damage by 25%. Fair Strike hits +2 more targets. (Requires 1 white star)

### Solar Shield

Gain % Crit and % Crit Resist. When deployed, the same bonus applies to all league heroes (including this hero).
- * Gain an extra 5% Amplify Damage of the Skill (Requires 3 gold stars)
- ** Gain an extra 10% Amplify Damage of the Skill (Requires 3 red stars)
- *** Gain an extra 10% Amplify Damage of the Skill (Requires 3 white stars)

### Sacred Burst

In battles with players, whenever an enemy unit within a certain range dies, the hero unleashes a wide-area attack that can hit up to 4 targets, dealing damage equal to % of Maximum Attack. Cooldown: 0.5 seconds.
Maximum Attack: During battles, when a hero loses HP, leading to a reduction in their troops, their Attack decreases proportionally. Maximum Attack refers to the value before this effect.
- * Increases skill damage by 27.5%. Each Sacred Burst  grants a shield equal to 3.5% of max HP and absorbs 60% of incoming damage for 6 seconds. Effects can stack. (Requires 5 gold stars)
- ** Skill damage increases by 27.5% (Requires 5 red stars)
- *** Increase skill damage by 27.5%. Sacred Burst hits +4 more targets. (Requires 3 white stars)

### Divine Descent

Ultimate Skill: Deal damage equals to % of the Attack to enemies in a wide area, then enter Possession mode. Attack increases by %, and normal attacks become area attacks. Lasts for 10 seconds. Cooldown: 21 seconds.
- * Increase skill damage by 35%. Boosts skill damage in Possession mode by 30% (Requires 2 gold stars)
- ** Skill damage increases by 35%. (Requires 2 red stars)
- *** Increase skill damage by 35%. In Possession mode, cooldown cap for Fair Strike and Confession is reduces by 50%. (Requires 5 white stars)

### Confession

Inflict damage equal to % of the Attack on 1 random enemies and apply a Vulnerable effect for 3 seconds. While vulnerable, the target's Skill Amplify Damage is reduced by 50%. Cooldown: 13 seconds. If the target is a summoned unit, damage is increased by 1000% and 70% of the cooldown is returned.
- * Increase skill damage by 40%. Also removes target's damage reduction effects. (Requires 4 gold stars)
- ** Skill damage increases by 40%. (Requires 4 red stars)
- *** Increase skill damage by 40%. Stuns target for 2 seconds after Vulnerable ends. (Requires 4 white stars)

### War Messenger

Increase soldier attack by % for every 1000 soldiers deployed. *Only one War Messenger works per team, their effects do not stack.

## Gear Skills


### Libram of Light

Bishop's Attacks apply a Divine Light mark to the enemy, reducting defense by 2% (up to 600 per stack), lasting 8 seconds and stacking up to 10 times.
- Lv. 5 Skill Damage increase to 10%
- Lv. 10 Boosts defense reduction by 2% (up to 1500 per stack). All the Bishop's attacks now apply the Divine Light mark.
- Lv. 15 At the beginning of the battle, boost all your League heroes' Attack by 10%
- Lv. 20 Boosts defense reduction by 2% (up to 2500 per stack). Each Divine Light stack also reduces enemy "damage increase" attribute by 2%.

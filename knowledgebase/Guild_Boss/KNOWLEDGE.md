# Guild Boss


## Selection Stage

1. After the event begins, the R4 or R5 of the guild must select the difficulty and time for the challenge.
2. Once the selection is made, the event progresses to thePreparation Stage.

## Preparation Stage

1. All guild members can donateCharm Arrowsto earn Guild points and increase the damage dealt to the Guild Boss.
2. Donations continue to earn Guild points even after reaching the maximum level, but the donation progress will reset when the current event cycle ends.
3. Charm Arrowscan be obtained from daily quests, with a maximum of 2 Charm Arrows available per day.

## Combat Stage

1. TheCombat Stagelasts for 30 minutes.
2. During this stage, lords can only attack the Guild Boss by sending rallies.
3. Guild members can send one team to initiate a rally, while other teams can join additional rallies as needed.
4. Rallies initiated by an R5 or officers receive a 10% damage boost, while rallies initiated by an R4 receive a 5% damage boost.
5. After theCombat Stageconcludes, rewards are calculated based on the total damage inflicted by the guild and individual members.

## Reward Settlement

1. The higher the total damage inflicted by the guild, the better the collective rewards for members, and higher difficulty challenges will be unlocked for future events.
2. The higher the individual damage inflicted by a lord, the better their personal rewards.
3. The lord who inflicts the highest damage has the opportunity to multiply the guild's collective rewards by a factor of 1 to 10 times, depending on their damage contribution.

## Difficulty Unlock

1. The Guild Boss has two states:Stage 1: Limited HP. Upon defeat, the guild can select a higher difficulty for the next event.Stage 2: Unlimited HP. Lords can continue attacking freely to inflict damage.

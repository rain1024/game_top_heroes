## Faction Counter System

Each faction has its own strengths and weaknesses, best illustrated by the diagram below:
- Horde has an advantage against League, but disadvantage against Nature
- Nature has an advantage against Horde, but disadvantage against League
- League has an advantage against Nature, but disadvantage against Horde
During battle, if a hero is dealing damage to another hero they have factional advantage over, then the damage is increased. Consequently, the damage received from a hero they have an advantage over is reduced.

### Traits

Each has four traits. To start upgrading a hero's traits, it needs first to be maxed out (5 silver stars). It costs diamonds to unlock a trait and shards to upgrade it. Traits need to be upgraded one by one, maxing out the first before doing the next. Each time it is possible to upgrade 5 levels before doing the next, and then it goes back to the first trait, until reaching level 30.
It costs 10 shards per level.

## Heroes Individual Roles

This section provides a detailed explanation of the hero's role, clarifying where they are best positioned and what theirspecific dutiesare within the game:
Damage Dealer:The heroes excel at dealing damage, increasing final damage by 3%.
Burst Striker:Specializes in delivering high burst damage in a short period through skills.
Area Damage Dealer:Excels at dealing Area of Effect (AOE) damage to multiple enemies.
Guardian:Positioned in the front row to absorb damage, reducing damage taken by 5%.
Controller:Capable of controlling enemies through crowd-control effects.
Sustainer:Inflicts special effects and engages in prolonged combat.
Buffer:Provides beneficial buffs to enhance teammates' performance.
Healer:Specializes in healing, enhancing healing effects by 5%.
Precision Striker:Focuses on dealing high damage to a single enemy.
Supporter:Offers strategic support by increasing teammates' maximum health by 5%.
Debuffer:Specializes in weakening enemies by applying debuffs.
Summoner:Capable of summoning additional units to assist in battle.
Understanding each hero's unique role is crucial for building a well-balanced team and executing effective strategies in the game. By recognizing the strengths and responsibilities of each role—whether it’s dealing damage, supporting allies, or controlling enemies—players can maximize their chances of success in various combat scenarios.

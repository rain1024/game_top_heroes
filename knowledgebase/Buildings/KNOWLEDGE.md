## League Statue

Unlocks Hero Tower at level 22, allowing the player to use excess Hero Shards to add Power, All Heroes HP, and other Faction Bonuses depending on the hero in the tower. Hero Shards can only be used in the tower once the hero reaches its maximum star level, with 10 Hero Shards being needed for each level. Per hero Faction Bonus levels are at lv. 50, 100, 150, 200, and 300.

| Hero | Bonus Description |
| --- | --- |
| Warrior | Carrying Capacity for All +1% |
| Wizard | Carrying Capacity for All +1% |
| Ranger | League Hero Attack +0.25% |
| Knight | League Hero HP +0.25% |
| Minister | League Hero HP +0.25% |

## Research Cottage

Used to research technologies in production, construction, and combat. Upgrading unlocks higher-level technologies. Also provides research boosting points and rewards received from theGuild Arms Raceevent.
The Research Cottage has a Winter skin which provides a boost of Tech Research Speed +15%.

## Warehouse

Used for storing resources and protecting more resources from being plundered. Increases the maximum resource storage limit after upgrading.
The Warehouse has a Winter skin which provides a boost of Warehouse Protection +20%.

## Watchtower

Unlocks extinguish feature at level 21, allowing scout queues to put out castle fires and restore some durability after an attack.

## Medal Hall

## Hospital

Used to heal injured soldiers after being wounded in battle. Healing 1 soldier costs 100 timber and 100 stone. When the hospital reaches maximum capacity, any soldiers injured in battle will instantly die.
The Hospital has a Winter skin which provides a boost of Hospital Capacity +15%.

# Shaman

The Shaman is a Legendary category healer hero in the Horde faction, specializing in healing, sustained combat, and multi-target damage/healing.
Hero Shards are unlocked in the Tavern at an early recruitment level.

## Skills


### Healing Breeze

Heal HP of 2 with the lowest HP equal to [x]% of Shaman's Attack. Cooldown: 8 seconds.
- ★ HP recovery increases by 51.25%.
- ★★ HP recovery increases by 51.25%.
- ★★★ HP recovery increases by 51.25%.

### Ancestor's Soul

Ultimate Skill: Summon ancestor's soul to possess the ally hero (excluding summoned units) with the lowest HP percentage, preventing death for [x] seconds. Cooldown: 24 seconds.
- ★ Increase effect duration by 0.35 seconds.
- ★★ Increase effect duration by 0.35 seconds.
- ★★★ Increase effect duration by 0.7 seconds.

### Rain Blessing

Increase all allies' healing effect by [x]% while Shaman is present.
- ★ Healing received effect increases by 1.25%.
- ★★ Healing received effect increases by 1.25%.
- ★★★ Healing received effect increases by 1.25%.

### Restorative Totem

Summon a healing totem that periodically restores HP equal to [x]% of Attack for 6 seconds. Cooldown: 15 seconds.
- ★ HP recovery increases by 20%.
- ★★ HP recovery increases by 20%.
- ★★★ HP recovery increases by 60%.

### Wolf Soul

When ancestor's soul is summoned, Shaman calls 2 wolves with [x]% of Shaman's attributes to join the battle for up to 8 seconds.
- ★ Increase the Attack and HP inherited by summoned units by 12.5%.
- ★★ Increase the Attack and HP inherited by summoned units by 12.5%.
- ★★★ Increase the Attack and HP inherited by summoned units by 12.5%.

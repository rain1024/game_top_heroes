# Wilderness Hunter

The Wilderness Hunter is a Legendary category hero in the Horde faction, specializing in damage dealing, sustained combat, and buffs.
Hero Shards unlock in the Tavern at recruitment Lv. 14.

## Gear Skills


## Awakening


#### Tier 1: Lv. 1 War Cry

Awakening Passive Skill. Increases the Attack and HP of carried soldiers by 20%.

#### Tier 2: Hunter's Instinct

Awakening Skill Enhancement Power up the Impulse Instinct. During battle, summons a black panther for coordinated attacks. The black panther inherits 100% of the Wilderness Hunter's attributes and gains an additional 40% dodge rate. The panther's revival time is 12 seconds. When the black panther is not present, the final damage of the Wilderness Hunter increases by 15%.

#### Tier 3: Lv. 2 War Cry

Awakening Passive Skill Upgrade. Increases the Attack and HP of carried soldiers by 35%.

#### Tier 4: Awakening: Wilderness Hunter

Unlock Wilderness Hunter's Awakening Skin: Improve hero rarity. Greatly increase attributes and troop limit.

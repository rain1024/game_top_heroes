Warrioris a frontline melee damage dealer and tank hybrid inTop Heroes. With high base health, strong counterattacks, and defensive utility, he’s a reliable pick for both early-game progression and PvP survival teams. He shines when absorbing damage and breaking enemy formations.

## Lore

Trained in the wilds and battlefields since youth, the Warrior stands at the front of every fight. With a sword forged in the heart of a fallen star and raw courage burning in his eyes, he charges into battle not for glory—but for those who cannot defend themselves.

## Skills

1. Blade Storm (Ultimate) 🌪️
Unleashes a whirlwind slash that hits all enemies in the front and middle rows.
→ Has a 30% chance to reduce enemy ATK by 20% for 2 turns.
2. Iron Defense 🛡️
Reduces damage taken by 35% for 2 turns and grants minor healing.
→ Great for tanking opening bursts.
3. Retaliate 🗡️
When hit, has a 50% chance to strike back for 120% of ATK.
→ Helps chip away at enemies during prolonged fights.
4. Unbreakable Spirit (Passive) 💪
Increases max HP by 10% and provides 15% tenacity.
→ Makes Warrior very durable in PvE and PvP.

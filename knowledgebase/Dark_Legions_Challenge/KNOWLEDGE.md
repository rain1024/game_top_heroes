# Dark Legion's Challenge

Dark Legion's Challengeis a regular event where the player can challengeDark Legionairesfor prizes.Before starting the challenge, the player has to pick a difficulty. In order to unlock the next difficulty, they have to defeat the legionaries 30 times at the lower difficulty.
